Vision

Tanta University is an Egyptian university located in Tanta in Gharbia governorate. It became a university in 1972 after it had been a branch of Alexandria University. Tanta University was established based on the republican decree No. 1468 of 1962. That decree stipulated founding Faculty of Medicine in Tanta. Currently, Tanta University includes 14 faculties and institutes namely faculties of Medicine, Science, Education, Commerce, Pharmacy, Dentistry, Arts, Law, Nursing, Engineering, Agriculture, Physical Education, Specific Education, and Computer and Information, in addition to the Technical Institute of Nursing and 37 special units

Mission

Tanta University abides by following a sustainable development strategy for improvement of the educational environment, production of knowledge,innovation, and entrepreneurship to cope with labour market needs in all sectors and to keep pace with the international advancement in competitive appliedresearch that addresses community issues.

Overview

Tanta University is a public university in Egypt located in the city of Tanta, which is the capital of Gharbia Governorate. It was founded in 1962 as a branch of Alexandria University, and later became an independent university in 1976. Tanta University offers undergraduate and graduate programs in various fields including medicine, engineering, agriculture, science, commerce, law, education, and arts. It has a number of faculties and institutes, including the Faculty of Medicine, Faculty of Engineering, Faculty of Agriculture, Faculty of Science, Faculty of Commerce, Faculty of Law, Faculty of Education, Faculty of Arts, and the Institute of Environmental Studies and Research. The university has a strong emphasis on research, and has several research centers and institutes, including the Center for Research and Development, the Center for Environmental Studies and Research, the Center for Genetic Engineering and Biotechnology, and the Center for Liver Disease Research. Tanta University has a diverse student body, with students coming from different parts of Egypt and other countries. The university provides various facilities and services to its students, including academic and career counseling, health services, sports facilities, and dormitories. Tanta University aims to provide quality education and contribute to the development of the society through its research programs and community outreach initiatives.
